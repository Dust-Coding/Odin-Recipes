Mustard Baked Chicken

Description

This healthy Mustard Baked Chicken Tenders recipe is super simple and deliciously flavorful. You can serve as-is, or dish it up over rice or a salad to soak up the tasty mustard sauce!


Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. chicken tenders
  • 1/2 cup whole grain mustard
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh tarragon
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425˚F (220˚C)
  2. Combine the mustard, tarragon, lemon juice, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl and mix well
  3. Pat dry the chicken tenders. Add the chicken to the bowl with mustard sauce and toss to coat each piece of chicken with the sauce.
  4. Transfer the chicken along with the sauce to a large baking dish. Cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is thoroughly cooked. Remove from oven and allow to rest for 5 minutes for juices to redistribute into the meat. Garnish with extra tarragon and serve.
